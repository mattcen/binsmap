Hello. Could we please have our waste collection zones on openbinmap.org? It's easy.
  1. Export the waste collection boundaries from your GIS as GeoJSON, following the format at opencouncildata.org.
  2. Upload the GeoJSON file to data.gov.au.
  3. Email okfnmelb@gmail.com when it's done.
